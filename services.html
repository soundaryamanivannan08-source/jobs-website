FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

How quickly can TalentBridge fill a position?
Our average time-to-fill is 14 working days for professional roles, versus the industry average of 42 days. For executive searches, it typically ranges from 3–6 weeks depending on the seniority and specialization required.
Do you offer a replacement guarantee?
Yes! All our placements come with a 90-day replacement guarantee. If a placed candidate leaves or isn't the right fit within the first 90 days, we will source and place a replacement at no additional charge.
Can candidates use TalentBridge for free?
Absolutely. TalentBridge is completely free for job seekers. You can create a profile, browse jobs, apply to positions, and access basic career guidance at no cost. Premium career coaching packages are available as optional paid add-ons.
What industries do you specialize in?
We have dedicated specialist teams covering 20+ verticals including Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Manufacturing, FMCG, Media, Legal, Education, Logistics, and more. Each recruiter has deep domain expertise in their assigned sector.
How does AI matching work?
Our AI engine analyzes 200+ data points per candidate including skills, experience trajectory, company culture fit, career goals, and compensation expectations. It then cross-references these against job requirements to generate a proprietary match score for each role.
What is your data privacy policy?
We are fully GDPR and PDPA compliant. All candidate data is stored securely, encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share personal data with third parties without explicit consent, and candidates can request data deletion at any time.
