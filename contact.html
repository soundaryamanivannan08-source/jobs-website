Support FAQ

Quick Answers

Common questions about reaching us and getting support.

What is your response time?
Our team typically responds within 2 business hours for email enquiries. Live chat responses are instant during operating hours (9 AM – 9 PM IST).
How do I report a technical issue?
Email support@talentbridge.in with "Technical Issue" in the subject line. Include your account email and a description of the problem. Screenshots are always helpful.
Can I schedule a demo for my company?
Absolutely! Select "Employer / Hiring" in the enquiry type above and mention you'd like a demo. Our enterprise sales team will reach out within 4 hours to book a convenient time.
Is there a physical office I can visit?
Yes — our headquarters is at 14th Floor, Prestige Tower, MG Road, Bangalore. We welcome visits by appointment. Please email us first to schedule a meeting.
